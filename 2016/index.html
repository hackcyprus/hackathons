Half a decade of coding.

Over the past four years, we introduced the first hackathon in Cyprus. We went from teaching people in Cyprus about the meaning of the word "hackathon", to being the organisers of the most anticipated weekend of hacking on hardware, mobile and the web.


For our 5th anniversary, we are revisiting CUT where it all began! Come join us for a celebratory hackathon where you'll get the chance to tinkle with awesome hardware, meet techies like you from all around Europe, build awesome stuff and of course have some birthday cake!

The Rules

You can design, sketch and conceptualise beforehand, but all code must be fresh.

You're welcome to come with a team or form a team on the day.Your team must be a maximum of 5 people.

You will have full ownership & IP of whatever you build.

Choose a good problem to solve or build something really awesome. Have fun!

FAQs

When is Hack{Cyprus}?

24 June - 26 June 2016

Wait, what's a hackathon?

Hackathons are events where people compete to build interesting technical projects in just over 36 hours. People come together, form teams, brainstorm, share ideas, and help each other build awesome things. All skill levels are highly welcome, so don't be intimidated if you don't feel your coding is up to scratch.

Is it really free?

Yep.

Even food?

Yes! Thanks to our amazing sponsors we will be offering meals, snacks and drinks to everyone.

Can I attend Hack{Cyprus}?

Everyone is welcome! As long as you are ready to bring your awesome idea to life and follow our simple guidelines you are good to come!

What time should I arrive?

The schedule can be found here.

Do I need to have a team?

You're welcome to make a team before the event, but loads of people come without a team. At the start of the event, we'll help everyone find a team. One of the best things about hackathons is meeting hackers from all over the country.

Who's organising Hack{Cyprus}?

Hack{Cyprus} Hackathons is organised by the Cypriot Enterprise Link.
