THE SUMMER EDITION

June 28-29th 2014 · Cyprus University of Technology

Get your free ticket

Put your hacking hat on and join 90+ creative developers, designers and ideators for 30 hours of hacking, designing, tinkering and general making of awesome things – web, mobile, hardware, anything goes.

You're welcome to come with a team or form a team on the day. We'll provide food, drinks and internet – mix in some creativity and skills and you have a recipe for getting that idea out of the back of your head and transforming it into bytes and pixels.

Prizes

The alchemist

You want to try out some cool new tech; drones, robots, vision, music controllers, gesture recognition, cool stuff.

You'll be judged on technical creativity and general awesomeness.

The problem solver

You want to tackle an actual problem; education, medicine, travel, green energy, health, important stuff.

You'll be judged on your solution to the problem and its marketability.

The crowd favourite

We'll let the crowd vote for this one; you win by getting the most love. Get ready to flirt.
